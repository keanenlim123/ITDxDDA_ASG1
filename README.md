# Krispy Anchovies
Krispy Anchovies is an AR app that teaches children how to use tools to clean aquatic habitats in a fun and interactive way. Learn about animals in the S.E.A. Aquarium and their environments by clearing trash from each habitat using tools such as the sponge and tongs.
Lure sea creatures out of hiding and interact with them through engaging AR animations. Complete quizzes and earn points that can be redeemed for exclusive rewards.

# System Requirements
- Processor: Mid-range or better from last 3 years
- RAM: Minimum 3GB, preferably 4GB+
- Storage: At least 32GB available space
- Camera: Good quality rear-facing camera
- Sensors: Gyroscope, accelerometer, magnetometer

# Technologies Used
- JSON – Used for displaying points and reward information.
- Firebase – Used for storing all player data, including scores, login details, trash-clearing time, and reward progression.
- Unity (AR Foundation) – Provides AR tracking, scene rendering, and tool interaction features.

# Controls & Gameplay Instructions
## Movement & Interaction
- Point your device at a flat surface to detect habitats.
- Tap to place the AR habitat in your environment.
- Move your device around to look at different parts of the habitat.

## Cleaning Tools
- Sponge: Swipe or tap the trash with the sponge to clean it.
- Tongs: Pinch or tap trash objects to grab and remove them.

## Creature Interaction
- Tap on sea creatures to make them perform animations.
- Lure creatures out by completing habitat-cleaning tasks.

## Quizzes & Rewards
- After cleaning a habitat, complete a short quiz.
- Earn points based on your score and speed.
- Redeem points for rewards in the Rewards section.

# Game Objective
Your mission is to restore all marine habitats by removing trash, helping animals return to a clean environment. Learn about real marine species, their diets, and their behaviors while playing. Complete all habitats, master all quizzes, and collect all available rewards.

# Known Bugs / Limitations
- User is able to get infinite points on the quizzes.
